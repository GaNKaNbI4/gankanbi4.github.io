About us

Alicia Perez

Director

LUNA is an innovative and dynamic DESIGN company based in Doha, Qatar since 2013. Our design allows us to offer our Clients unique and exclusive spaces featuring pure elegance and quality. We take every project as a new challenge, interpreting the needs and wishes of the client. LUNA expertise is extensive in all the areas as residential, hospitality, workspace, retail, branding and tailor made furniture. LUNA was born to create a new way of understanding the world of design, furniture, interior and exterior decoration.

Luna passion is the design!

Services

Interior Design

Creating Inspiring environments and deeply satisfying experiences.

Luna's approach fuses outstanding creativity and commercial reasoning with imagination, simplicity and quality.

Master Planning

Shaping the future of urban areas by creating resilient highly livable cities.

At Luna, we combine our in-depth understanding of communities, placemaking and urban development with market understanding and design creativity.

Landscape Designs

Creating memorable spaces with a strong identity.

Luna's Landscape Architecture capabilities cross all aspects of the urban environment. We take a pro-active approach towards design, guiding and influencing the decision making process to create the very best in landscape.

Architecture

Creating beautiful places and spaces that interact with the lives of those that use them.

Working together we create defendable design narratives which produce finishes places with a "must have" quality

Project Management + Fit Out

Inspired by nature, unique design pieces which is forged by skilled craftsmen.

At Luna, we merge our design ideas and transform them to a reality in conjunction with teams of skillful people.

Bespoke Furniture + Lighting

Time, cost and quality must be always present in the projects.

Luna provides great expertise for the construction stage of the project to ensure the clients the design is achieved and with the expectations by the client.
